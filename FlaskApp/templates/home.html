National Hockey League (NHL) Fantasy Sports Lineup

Our motivation is to equip various fantasy players, from casual players to the stats enthusiast, with improved analytics and effective visualizations to help them design optimal lineups. Daily fantasy sports (DFS) is estimated to be over a billion dollar industry. With our NHL (National Hockey League) DFS product, we have the ability to disrupt this sector.

Lineup Optimizer

Shows all of the player DFS prices and predictions, and enables you to optimize your lineup. Shows DFS performance of your optimized lineup.

Player Stats

Shows the last 10 game performance of every player. Allows you to dig in beyond the optimal lineup.

Data Source

All data is pulled directly from the NHL API. Documentation available at: https://gitlab.com/dword4/nhlapi/-/tree/master/
